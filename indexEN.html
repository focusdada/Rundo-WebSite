Welcome to Rundo International Pharmaceutical Research & Development Co., Ltd.

Founded in 2004, Rundo International Pharmaceutical Research & Development Co., Ltd. (Rundo) is one of the top Chinese CROs specialized in providing Professional Clinical Research Outsourcing Services to Domestic and International clients. Rundo was one of the main promoters in the establishment of the Chinese CRO Union (CROU). Now, we are the Chairman Unit of the CROU and also the first Chinese CRO to obtain ISO9001:2008 accreditation plus CRO codes of practice certificate.

Company Overview

Rundo is based in Shanghai with branches located in Beijing and a subsidiary clinical data management (CDM) centre in Nantong. Additionally, Rundo has offices and home-based staff in more than 20 provinces around China. Over the past 10 years since establishment, Rundo has carried out over 480 trials covering a wide range of Therapeutic Areas (TA) including Oncology, Cardiology, Respiratory, Gastroenterology, Endocrinology, CNS, Anti-Infection and many others. Our Nantong CDM centre has undertaken a vast number of Data Management and Drug Safety Data Management services, gaining praise from our Sponsors from America, Europe and Japan with high quality and efficiency.

In order to meet the increasing demand for Pac-Asia clinical trials, Rundo, in collaboration with the other core countries and regions, including Japan, Korea, Taiwan etc. - established the Alliance for Pac-Asia Clinical Trials (A-PACT). After a three-year trial run, A-PACT was officially established in Shanghai in May, 2012. With unified SOPs and Project Management approach, A-PACT has already won the interest of numerous Sponsors, offering high quality, efficient and flexible Regional solutions at reasonable costs.

Being at the forefront of the pharmaceutical industry, Rundo provides its Sponsors with high quality services compliant with ICH-GCP, CFDA-GCP and other related regulations. By virtue of its own strength and recognition from Sponsors, Rundo has become one of the leading CROs in the Chinese CRO industry. Our business has already extended outside the China market and into the rest of Pac-Asia as well as Europe and America.
